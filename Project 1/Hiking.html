Ice climbing & hiking on glaciers

Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers and easy glacier hike on Solheimajokull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Solheimajokull glaicer. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come experience this amazing crawling glaicer with us.

Solheimajokull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Myralsjokull and is surrouded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glaiceronly adding to the charms of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
